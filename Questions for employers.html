1.      What are your nurse to patient ratios? And do you have a maximum number of patient that can be assigned to a nurse?

2.      What does your orientation process for new nurses to the unit include?

3.      What is the organizations policy on floating nurses to other units?

4.      What is the average time it take a nurse to move from being a level 1 nurse to a level 2 nurse at the facility?

5.      Are mentorship opportunities available?

6.      What are the qualities that you are looking for in a new nurse?
