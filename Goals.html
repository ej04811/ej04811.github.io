Short Term Goals:

·         To pass the NCLEX on the first attempt.

·         To obtain my first job in an acute care setting. With a time frame of 1 – 3 months from graduation.

·         To maintain my BLS certification and obtain my ACLS, PALS and NRP within six months.

·         Be successful in a new nurse residency program.

·         To start working on ECU’s as soon as I obtain my license and keep a online folder of the work.

 

Long Term Goals:

·         To get a job in the ICU.

·         To earn my CCRN certification

·         Become a member of the rapid response team.

·         To complete a master’s degree program to become a nurse practitioner.

·         Explore other areas of nursing and obtain certifications in areas I have an interest in.
